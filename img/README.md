# Figure folder — "the internet, as children drew it"

The Beat-2 gallery (`#metaphor` in `index.html`) shows the children's drawings sorted
oldest→newest. **Three of the four are cleared and hosted here**; the gallery prints full
provenance under every plate (keep it). The earliest figure — Thatcher & Greyling (1998),
© Elsevier — is **deliberately held out** and rendered as a credited "kintsugi" gap rather
than reposted (see the builder's note in `index.html`).

Licence call (made for this deck, kept on the public site):

- **Botturi & Addimando (2025)** — open access / CC BY: free to reproduce with credit.
- **Kodama et al. (2017)** — © Springer, but shown from the authors' own copy, with full credit.
- **Nesta / De Roeck (2019)** — outreach project, no explicit licence; shown with credit as
  illustrative material (and the live gallery is one click away in the deck).
- **Thatcher & Greyling (1998)** — © Elsevier: **not hosted.** Held out on purpose.
